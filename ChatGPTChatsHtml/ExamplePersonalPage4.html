It was this initial love for gaming that eventually got me fascinated by machine learning and AI. How do game engines work? What's behind the NPC's decision-making? These questions led me to pursue a career in AI.

When I'm not working or gaming, you'll find me training for my next marathon. Running offers a kind of meditative solace, a contrast to the fast-paced world of tech and gaming.
